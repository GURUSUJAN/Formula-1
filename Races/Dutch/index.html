Circuit Zandvoort

First Grand Prix: 1952

Laps: 72

Circuit Length: 4.259 km

Race Distance: 306.587 km

Lap Record: 1:11.097 (Lewis Hamilton, Mercedes, 2021)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Zandvoort, Netherlands
  • Constructed: 1948
  • Track Features: Historic circuit with a challenging layout, including banked corners and fast, sweeping bends
  • Why Go? Experience the atmosphere of a classic circuit surrounded by sand dunes and the North Sea coastline, with a passionate Dutch crowd
  • Best Viewing Spot: Gold 3 grandstand offers views of the iconic Tarzan corner and the start/finish straight, while the dunes around Turns 6 and 7 provide unique vantage points
