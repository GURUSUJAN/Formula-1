Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez

First Grand Prix: 1963

Laps: 71

Circuit Length: 4.304 km

Race Distance: 305.354 km

Lap Record: 1:17.774 (Valtteri Bottas, Mercedes, 2021)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Mexico City, Mexico
  • Constructed: 1962
  • Track Features: High altitude circuit, famous for its unique stadium section and the long main straight
  • Why Go? Experience the vibrant atmosphere of the Mexican Grand Prix, enjoy the unique track layout, and explore the rich cultural heritage of Mexico City
  • Best Viewing Spot: Grandstand 12 offers a great view of the cars accelerating out of the stadium section
