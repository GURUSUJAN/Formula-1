Yas Marina Circuit

First Grand Prix: 2009

Laps: 58

Circuit Length: 5.281km

Race Distance: 306.183 km

Lap Record: 1:26.103 (Max Verstappen, Red Bull Racing, 2021)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates
  • Constructed: 2009
  • Track Features: Iconic twilight race with state-of-the-art facilities, featuring long straights, tight corners, and a unique pit exit under a hotel
  • Why Go? Experience the excitement of F1 racing at the Yas Marina Circuit, enjoy the luxurious hospitality, and explore the vibrant city of Abu Dhabi
  • Best Viewing Spot: North Grandstand offers views of the first corner, the long straight, and the cars navigating through the marina section
