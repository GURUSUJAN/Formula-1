Autódromo José Carlos Pace

First Grand Prix: 1973

Laps: 71

Circuit Length: 4.309 km

Race Distance: 305.879 km

Lap Record: 1:10.540 (Valtteri Bottas, Mercedes, 2018)

Brief Info:

  • Location: São Paulo, Brazil
  • Constructed: 1940 (as Interlagos), renovated in 1990
  • Track Features: Anti-clockwise layout with several high-speed corners and elevation changes
  • Why Go? Experience the passionate atmosphere of the Brazilian Grand Prix, enjoy the challenging track layout, and explore the vibrant city of São Paulo
  • Best Viewing Spot: Grandstand A offers a great view of the start/finish straight and Turn 1, a popular overtaking spot
