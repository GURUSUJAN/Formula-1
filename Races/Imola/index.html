Imola Circuit

First Grand Prix: 1980

Laps: 63

Circuit Length: 4.909 km

Race Distance: 309.049 km

Lap Record: 1:15.484 (Lewis Hamilton, Mercedes, 2020)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Imola, Italy
  • Constructed: 1950
  • Track Features: Historic circuit with challenging corners, elevation changes, and a fast layout
  • Why Go? Experience the thrill of racing at one of the most iconic and historic circuits on the F1 calendar, and explore the beautiful countryside of the Emilia Romagna region
  • Best Viewing Spot: Variante Alta offers great views of cars negotiating the chicane and the exit onto the main straight, providing plenty of overtaking opportunities
