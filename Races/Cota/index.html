Circuit of The Americas

First Grand Prix: 2012

Laps: 56

Circuit Length: 5.513 km

Race Distance: 308.405 km

Lap Record: 1:36.169 (Charles Leclerc, Scuderia Ferrari, 2019)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Austin, Texas, USA
  • Constructed: 2012
  • Track Features: Anti-clockwise layout with challenging corners, elevation changes, and a blend of high-speed and low-speed sections
  • Why Go? Experience the excitement of F1 racing at the state-of-the-art Circuit of The Americas, enjoy the vibrant atmosphere in Austin, and explore the rich cultural heritage of Texas
  • Best Viewing Spot: Main Grandstand offers panoramic views of the start-finish straight and Turn 1, while the Grandstand at Turns 12 and 15 provides close-up views of the cars navigating the challenging esses and hairpin turns
