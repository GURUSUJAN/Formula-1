Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya

First Grand Prix: 1991

Laps: 66

Circuit Length: 4.675 km

Race Distance: 307.236 km

Lap Record: 1:16.330 (Max Verstappen, Red Bull Racing, 2023)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Barcelona, Spain
  • Constructed: 1991
  • Track Features: Mix of high-speed corners and technical sections
  • Why Go? Experience the thrill of the Spanish Grand Prix at the Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya, known for its challenging layout and frequent pre-season testing, and explore the vibrant city of Barcelona
  • Best Viewing Spot: Grandstand L offers views of the cars navigating through the tricky final sector, including the challenging chicane at Turns 14 and 15, and the start-finish straight
