Autodromo Nazionale Monza

First Grand Prix: 1950

Laps: 53

Circuit Length: 5.793 km

Race Distance: 306.72 km

Lap Record: 1:21.046 (Rubens Barrichello, Ferrari, 2004)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Monza, Italy
  • Constructed: 1922
  • Track Features: Historic and high-speed circuit with long straights, fast corners, and minimal run-off areas
  • Why Go? Experience the atmosphere of one of the oldest and most iconic race tracks in the world, known for its passionate Tifosi and thrilling racing action
  • Best Viewing Spot: Grandstand 6 offers views of the start/finish line and the pit lane, while grandstands around the Ascari chicane provide excellent vantage points for overtaking action
