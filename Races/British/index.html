Silverstone Circuit

First Grand Prix: 1950

Laps: 52

Circuit Length: 5.891 km

Race Distance: 306.198 km

Lap Record: 1:27.097 (Max Verstappen, Red Bull Racing, 2020)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Silverstone, Northamptonshire, England
  • Constructed: 1943
  • Track Features: High-speed circuit with a mix of fast corners, including the famous Maggots, Becketts, and Chapel complex
  • Why Go? Witness history at the home of British motorsport and experience the thrill of F1 racing at one of the fastest circuits on the calendar
  • Best Viewing Spot: Grandstand Luffield offers views of the challenging Luffield complex, while the Becketts grandstands provide an excellent vantage point to witness the high-speed action
