Shanghai International Circuit

First Grand Prix:2004

Laps: 56

Circuit Length: 5.451km

Race Distance: 305.066 km

Lap Record:1:32.238 (Michael Schumacher, Scuderia Ferrari, 2004)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Shanghai, China
  • Constructed: April 2003
  • Track Features: Unique design with high-speed corners, long straights, and challenging hairpins
  • Why Go? Experience the dynamic and lively city of Shanghai, enjoy the race action, and explore the hip bars and amazing restaurants in the sprawling Pudong district
  • Best Viewing Spot: Grandstand K for views of the Turn 14-15 hairpin or Turn 6 for great overtaking action
