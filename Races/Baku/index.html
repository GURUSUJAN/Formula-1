Baku City Circuit

First Grand Prix: 2016

Laps: 51

Circuit Length: 6.003 km

Race Distance: 306.049 km

Lap Record: 1:43.009 (Charles Leclerc, Ferrari, 2019)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Baku, Azerbaijan
  • Constructed: 2016
  • Track Features: Street circuit with long straights, tight corners, and a castle section
  • Why Go? Experience the unique blend of modern and historic surroundings in the heart of Baku, and enjoy the thrilling action of F1 racing on the city streets
  • Best Viewing Spot: Grandstand I provides panoramic views of the main straight and the pit lane, while grandstands around Turns 1 and 2 offer close-up views of the cars tackling the challenging first sector
