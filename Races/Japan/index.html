Suzuka International Racing Course

First Grand Prix:1987

Laps: 53

Circuit Length: 5.807km

Race Distance: 307.471 km

Lap Record: 1:30.983 (Lewis Hamilton , Mercedes, 2019)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Suzuka, Japan
  • Constructed: 1962
  • Track Features: High-speed circuit with iconic corners like the 'S' Curves, Degners, and 130R
  • Why Go? Experience one of F1's seminal tracks, witness legendary moments, enjoy the unique fan atmosphere, and indulge in great food
  • Best Viewing Spot: Grandstands D and E for views of the 'S' Curves, Grandstand G for inside 130R, or Grandstand I for overtaking action at the Hairpin
