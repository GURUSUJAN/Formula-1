Marina Bay Street Circuit

First Grand Prix: 2008

Laps: 62

Circuit Length: 4.94km

Race Distance: 306.143 km

Lap Record: 1:35.867 (Lewis Hamilton, Mercedes, 2023)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Marina Bay, Singapore
  • Constructed: 2008
  • Track Features: Illuminated night race with tight corners, high-speed straights, and a unique waterfront setting
  • Why Go? Experience the thrilling atmosphere of F1 racing under the lights in one of the most iconic street circuits, and explore the vibrant city of Singapore with its rich cultural heritage and modern skyline
  • Best Viewing Spot: Esplanade Waterfront Grandstand offers panoramic views of the Marina Bay area, while the Bay Grandstand provides close-up views of the cars racing along the waterfront and through the tight turns of the circuit
