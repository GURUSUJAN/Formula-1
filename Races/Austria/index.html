Red Bull Ring

First Grand Prix: 1970

Laps: 71

Circuit Length: 4.318 km

Race Distance: 306.452 km

Lap Record: 1:05.619 (Carlos Sainz.Jr, McLaren, 2020)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Spielberg, Austria
  • Constructed: 1969
  • Track Features: Fast and undulating track with elevation changes and tight corners
  • Why Go? Experience the unique atmosphere of the Austrian Grand Prix at the Red Bull Ring, known for its stunning alpine backdrop and thrilling racing action
  • Best Viewing Spot: Grandstand T3/T5 offers views of the cars navigating through the tight Turn 3 and Turn 5, providing excellent overtaking opportunities
