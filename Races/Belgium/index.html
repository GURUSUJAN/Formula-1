Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps

First Grand Prix: 1950

Laps: 44

Circuit Length: 7.004 km

Race Distance: 308.052 km

Lap Record: 1:46.286 (Valtteri Bottas, Mercedes, 2018)

Brief Info:

  • Location: Stavelot, Belgium
  • Constructed: 1921
  • Track Features: Legendary and historic track with challenging corners and high-speed sections, including the iconic Eau Rouge and Raidillon
  • Why Go? Experience the thrill of one of the most iconic tracks in motorsport history, surrounded by beautiful Ardennes forest scenery
  • Best Viewing Spot: Gold 9 grandstand offers views of the start/finish straight, while the iconic Eau Rouge section can be observed from the General Admission area at the top of the hill
